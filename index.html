Welcome to Tic-Tac-Toe Game...

Instructions

  1. Players: There are two players, one uses "X" and the other uses "O".
  2. Turns: Players take turns marking an empty spot on the grid with their symbol ("X" or "O").
  3. Grid: The game board is a 3x3 grid with 9 spaces in total.
  4. Winning: The first player to align three of their symbols in a row (horizontally, vertically, or diagonally) wins the game.
  5. Tie: If all the spaces are filled and no player has won, the game ends in a draw.
